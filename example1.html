Our Story

Where Smoke Meets Soul

Founded in 2023, Aura & Ember was born from a passion for elemental cooking. We believe that fire is an ingredient, not just a method.

Our chefs source local, seasonal ingredients and transform them over open wood embers to create flavors that are both primal and sophisticated.
