Structure & Revisions

Launchpad vs Professional Layouts

The Launchpad tier is a high-converting, single-page scroll layout containing up to 5 specific sections (e.g., Hero, About, Services, Testimonials, Contact). It’s designed to funnel customers straight to your inbox.

The Professional tier unlocks a multi-page architecture (up to 5 separate pages) with persistent sticky navigation, dedicated FAQ sections, and categorized service menus.

The Revision Guarantee

We are confident in our designs. We will build you a free initial prototype to review. Once you commit to a subscription, we offer 3 rounds of free major revisions to fine-tune the site before we officially launch it to the public.

Post-launch, you'll use your dedicated monthly update hours (1 hour on Launchpad, 3 hours on Pro) for ongoing edits like swapping images or changing service prices.
